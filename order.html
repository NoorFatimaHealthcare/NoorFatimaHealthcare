Frequently asked questions

Do I need a prescription?
For most OTC medicines no prescription is required. For prescription-only medicines it helps to send a photo of the prescription on WhatsApp for faster processing.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash on delivery, UPI, and online payment links when available. Confirm payment method while placing your order.
How quickly will my order arrive?
Delivery times vary by area. We aim for same-day delivery in nearby zones and next-day in remote areas. We'll share an estimated time when confirming your order.
