Our Story

From a small team of passionate professionals to a trusted healthcare partner—our journey began with one simple belief: everyone deserves easy access to medicine.

How It All Started

In 2010, Noor Fatima Healthcare Group was founded by two pharmacists in a modest clinic. They saw families struggling to find genuine medicine and vowed to change that.

Over the years, we grew our network, embraced technology, and built partnerships to ensure fast, reliable delivery to every doorstep.

Our Milestones

2012: Launched our first online ordering platform.

2015: Expanded to 50+ cities across the region.

2020: Introduced real-time tracking and teleconsultation services.

Today, we continue to innovate—always guided by our founding promise to bring healthcare home.
